# Regression-Based Estimation of Causal Effects in the Presence of Selection Bias and Confounding

We derive theoretical results and propose a two-step regression estimator (TSR) to recover and identify causal effects for a continuous target under both selection bias and confounding, leveraging external data and proxy variables.
